## Product identity

| Dimension | Decision |
|---|---|
| Working name | **NightTable CO** |
| Positioning | **B5 + B2 + B3** — Premium Food & Nightlife in Colombia |
| Business model | **P4** hybrid: marketplace + venue SaaS + booking + reviews + billing + nightlife |
| Functional v1 | **F-Full** (see plan) |

| Infra v1 | **I1** Railway/Render + managed PG + Redis |

## Promise

Discover and reserve **high-rated, recognized** restaurants, bars, and clubs across Colombia. Night is a first-class experience (not a dining afterthought).

## Quality bar (B5)

Default discovery surfaces only venues that pass `quality_score` (rating × volume + curation badge). Home never dumps the raw ingested catalog.
